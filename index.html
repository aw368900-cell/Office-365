Is this really a one-time payment?
Yes. You pay once and receive a lifetime account. There are no monthly or yearly renewal fees. Your access remains active as long as the account exists.

Yes. All accounts are 100% genuine and verified by Microsoft. You will log in directly at setup.office.com or through the official Office apps to activate.
What apps are included in the suite?
The suite includes Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, OneNote, Publisher (PC only), and Access (PC only), plus 1TB of OneDrive cloud storage.
Can I use this on my Mac and iPad?
Yes! This license works on Windows, macOS, iPadOS, iOS, and Android. You can be signed into 5 devices at once.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you encounter any technical issues that our support team cannot resolve.
